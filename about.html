Who We Are

At the AVABODH Foundation, we are committed to driving meaningful change by uniting corporates, NGOs, and colleges in impactful collaborations.

Our mission is to ignite transformative CSR initiatives by connecting businesses with the ideal NGO partners, ensuring every project creates a tangible difference. Through our flagship Avabodh Foundation Internship Program, students gain a unique opportunity to participate in hands-on, on-the-ground events.

Our Mission

We unite people to drive positive social change. Connecting companies, NGOs, and colleges, we empower students to work on impactful solutions. Collaboration is the key to success.

Our Vision

We envision a world where teamwork creates lasting change. AVABODH Foundation brings together companies, NGOs, colleges, and volunteers to tackle key challenges and build resilient communities and a fair, sustainable world.
