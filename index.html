Who We Are

At the AVABODH Foundation, we are committed to driving meaningful change by uniting corporates, NGOs, and colleges in impactful collaborations. Our mission is to ignite transformative CSR initiatives by connecting businesses with the ideal NGO partners, ensuring every project creates a tangible difference.

Through our flagship Avabodh Foundation Internship Program, students gain a unique opportunity to participate in hands-on, on-the-ground events.
