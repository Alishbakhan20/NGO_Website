Program Overview

The Avabodh Foundation Internship is designed to give you real-world experience while deeply contributing to social causes. Our flexible program caters to students wanting to build their portfolios while creating a real impact on our society.

  • Duration: 3–6 months
  • Commitment: 120 hours minimum
  • Weekly: 8–10 hours/week

Learning Objectives

  • Gain hands-on experience in your selected domain within a real organizational context.
  • Develop empathy and awareness regarding key social issues outlined by the United Nations SDGs.
  • Enhance communication, teamwork, and leadership skills through on-the-ground events and remote coordination.
  • Build a professional network with corporate leaders, NGO heads, and fellow proactive student volunteers.

Roles & Responsibilities

Depending on the domain chosen, responsibilities will vary widely. In general, all interns are expected to:

  • Actively participate in domain-specific tasks assigned by coordinators.
  • Maintain regular communication and provide weekly progress reports.
  • Commit fully to timelines and project deadlines.
  • Represent the organization positively in digital or physical campaigns.
  • Collaborate effectively with other departments to create comprehensive outputs.
